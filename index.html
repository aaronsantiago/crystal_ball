click this to open configuration help

all changes to configuration on this page are saved on your browser as soon as you change them.

there are two sets that can be configured independently, they each have different files and different entries in the configuration panel in the top right. they are labelled 1 and 2. click the section in the configuration panel to expand it and see the options.

the videos are loaded from the videos/ folder inside the crystal ball server folder.

this webapp must be run in google chrome.

instructions for use:

  1. open this page in two tabs
  2. put each tab on its own monitor screen
  3. on the first monitor, click "use set 1 videos with camera" and choose the corresponding camera
  4. on the second monitor, click "use set 2 videos with camera" and choose the corresponding camera
    • if you do not see multiple camera buttons under the "use set videos with camera" section, make sure they are plugged in and refresh the page
  5. on the configuration panel in the top right, click "fullscreen" on both tabs
